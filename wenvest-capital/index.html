    About

    The Entrepreneurial Investor for Growth

    WENVEST Capital is a private investment firm, founded by Bernd Wendeln. Having started with traditional Angel Investments, WENVEST Capital emerged to become an entrepreneurial Venture Capital investor for Growth. The team of WENVEST Capital is dedicated to support the digital leaders of tomorrow with capital, know-how and network.

    We are based in Munich and are looking for investment opportunities primarily in Germany but also across other European countries.

    Team

    Your Partners for Capital & Expansion

    Bernd Wendeln
    Managing Partner

    Bernd has invested in about 40 start-ups over the past 20 years. He enjoys working with ambitious entrepreneurs and has been on numerous boards. He co-founded and developed the family office WEGA Invest since the year 2000. Bernd is also passionate about impact investing and is co-founder and chairman of the social venture capital fund BonVenture in Munich.

    Apart from work, he enjoys sports (tennis, ski, triathlon etc.), motorcycling, good wine and of course time with his family.

    Christophe Aumaître
    Principal

    Christophe has been active in the investment area since more than 10 years and has made >20 investments across different stages and sizes globally in technology / digital. He also gained significant operational experience including board roles as managing director of Jochen Schweizer GmbH and led major integration & transformation processes. Christophe is motivated and driven by working with passionate entrepreneurs as well as fast moving start-/scale-ups disrupting traditional business models.

    Christophe’s newest passion is road cycling and enjoys spending time with his family with good food & wine.
